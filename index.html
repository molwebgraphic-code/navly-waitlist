How it works

Three taps before you
pull away. Nothing else changes.

Step 01 · Before

Tap record. Drive.

One button on your home screen. Navly listens to the lesson — filtering engine noise and background chatter — and captures only your instructor's feedback. No extra hardware. No app on their phone.

On-device audio Chrome + Edge
Step 02 · During

AI tags every fault.

Navly understands the DVSA fault list — the same one your examiner uses. Every time your instructor says "check your mirror" or "more gas", it's tagged, timestamped, and counted. Automatically.

27 DVSA categories Live tagging
Step 03 · After

Know your readiness score.

A number — based on your last five lessons, which faults are repeating, and what the DVSA weighs most. When it hits 85%, you book. Not before. No more guessing if you're ready.
